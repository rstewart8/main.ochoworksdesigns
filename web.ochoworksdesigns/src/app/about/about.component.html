About OchoWorks Designs

Since 1995, we have been dedicated to helping future homeowners, general contractors, and developers bring residential projects to life through exceptional architectural design and drafting services. With over three decades of experience in the design-build industry, OchoWorks Designs was established to provide specialized expertise in residential home design, combining artistic creativity with practical construction knowledge.

Randy & Jen - Founders

Randy, a graduate of Brigham Young University with a degree in Construction Management, brings invaluable real-world building experience to every project. His comprehensive understanding of the construction process ensures that our designs are not only aesthetically pleasing but also practical and buildable. Jen contributes her exceptional design expertise, collaborating closely with clients to transform their ideas and sketches into beautiful, functional home plans that perfectly suit their lifestyle and vision. Together, they have cultivated a deep understanding of what contractors require for accurate pricing and what building officials need for seamless permit approval.

Quinn - CAD Drafting Specialist

Quinn joined our team approximately a year ago and has quickly become an integral part of our operations. He expertly manages the technical CAD drafting aspects of our projects, ensuring precision and attention to detail in every plan we deliver.

Our Services

Homeowners

Whether you're starting with preliminary sketches, collected inspiration, or one of our featured plans, we'll guide you through the entire design process. We create comprehensive plan sets that include site plans, foundation layouts, floor framing specifications, architectural elevations, electrical plans, roof details, and wall sections—everything needed to transform your dream home into reality.

General Contractors & Developers

We provide professional, construction-ready plan sets with clear specifications and buildable details that streamline client builds, spec homes, and residential development projects. Our services include access to our featured plans, custom plan modifications to meet specific site requirements or client preferences, full custom design collaboration, and ongoing technical support throughout construction. All plans include CAD DWG files for seamless engineering integration.

Our team is committed to delivering exceptional designs that are both beautiful and practical. We pride ourselves on creating permit-ready plans that meet the highest standards of quality and professionalism, whether you're a future homeowner planning your dream home or a contractor/developer seeking reliable, construction-ready documentation.
