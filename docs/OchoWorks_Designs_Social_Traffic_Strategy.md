# OchoWorks Designs Social Traffic Strategy

Prepared April 26, 2026

## Goal

Use Facebook, Instagram, and Pinterest as one connected traffic system that moves people from discovery to trust to website visits, quote requests, and sales.

Instagram should create interest and showcase taste. Facebook should build trust, local awareness, and retargeting audiences. Pinterest should create long-term search traffic that can keep sending people to the website for months.

## 1. Set Up The Traffic Foundation

### Claim and connect accounts

- Claim the OchoWorks Designs website on Pinterest. This connects saved Pins from the website back to the Pinterest profile and unlocks better analytics.
- Confirm the Pinterest account is a business account so OchoWorks Designs can use Pinterest Analytics and ads.
- Claim the Instagram account on Pinterest and enable auto-publishing where appropriate. This lets new Instagram posts become Pins automatically.
- Make sure Instagram, Facebook, and Pinterest all point to the same primary website destination.

### Add tracking

- Add Meta Pixel to the website so Facebook and Instagram traffic can be measured.
- Add Meta Conversions API if possible. This improves measurement and gives Meta better data for ad optimization.
- Use UTM links for every platform so Google Analytics or another analytics tool can show where traffic came from.

Example links:

- `?utm_source=instagram&utm_medium=social&utm_campaign=portfolio`
- `?utm_source=facebook&utm_medium=social&utm_campaign=local_awareness`
- `?utm_source=pinterest&utm_medium=pin&utm_campaign=custom_designs`

## 2. Instagram Strategy

Use Instagram to create desire, show proof, and make the brand feel active and trustworthy.

### What to post

- Finished project photos
- Before and after transformations
- Short Reels showing the design or build process
- Behind-the-scenes studio or workshop content
- Customer testimonials
- Seasonal design ideas
- Product or service spotlights

### How to drive traffic

- Put the best current website link in the profile bio. Do not default to the homepage if a portfolio, shop, contact, or quote-request page is more useful.
- Use Story link stickers for new projects, promotions, blog posts, and service pages.
- Pin three strong posts to the profile: an introduction, a proof or portfolio piece, and a direct offer.
- Give each Reel or carousel a clear call to action, such as "See the full project on our site" or "Request a custom design quote."

### Weekly target

- 3 Reels
- 2 feed posts or carousels
- Stories on active workdays

## 3. Facebook Strategy

Use Facebook for trust, local visibility, project storytelling, and retargeting.

### What to post

- Project albums
- Testimonials
- Local announcements
- Availability updates
- Event or market notices
- Longer stories about custom work
- Finished project reveals

### How to drive traffic

- Share every new website page, portfolio item, product, or blog post to the Facebook business page.
- Create photo albums by category, such as Custom Designs, Before and After, Available Now, and Client Projects.
- Participate in relevant local, home, design, and community groups through a personal profile. Add value first and share links only when relevant.
- Boost posts only after they show organic engagement.

### Ads

- Use Meta's Traffic objective when the goal is to send people to a website page.
- Use Sales or conversion-focused campaigns when there is a clear website action, such as purchases, quote requests, or contact form submissions.
- Start small, such as $5 to $10 per day for 7 days, and compare cost per landing page view, quote request, and purchase.

## 4. Pinterest Strategy

Pinterest should be treated like a visual search engine. It is likely the strongest long-term traffic opportunity for OchoWorks Designs because Pins can keep circulating and ranking after the day they are posted.

### Board ideas

- Custom Wood Designs
- Handmade Home Decor
- Modern Rustic Decor
- Gift Ideas
- Custom Signs
- Home Office Design
- Entryway Decor
- Kitchen Decor Ideas
- Wedding and Anniversary Gifts
- Colorado Custom Design, if locally relevant

### Pin strategy

- Create multiple Pins for every important website page.
- Use vertical images, ideally a 2:3 ratio.
- Add simple text overlays that match what people search for, such as "Custom Entryway Bench" or "Handmade Business Sign."
- Write keyword-rich Pin titles and descriptions.
- Link each Pin directly to the most relevant website page, not always the homepage.
- Make 5 to 10 Pin variations for each strong project or product.

### Weekly target

- 5 to 10 fresh Pins per week at minimum
- More during seasonal buying periods, product launches, or portfolio updates

## 5. Repurpose One Project Into Many Assets

One strong project can become:

- 1 website portfolio page
- 1 Instagram Reel
- 1 Instagram carousel
- 3 Instagram Stories
- 1 Facebook album
- 1 Facebook storytelling post
- 5 Pinterest Pins
- 1 testimonial graphic, if the client provides feedback

This is the core leverage point. Do not create completely different content for every platform. Create one strong project story, then adapt it to the format each platform prefers.

## 6. Suggested 14-Day Launch Plan

### Days 1-2: Website and tracking

- Confirm the website has clear pages for portfolio, services/products, and contact or quote requests.
- Add or verify Meta Pixel.
- Add UTM tracking conventions.
- Make sure each social profile points to the best website destination.

### Days 3-4: Pinterest setup

- Confirm Pinterest business account.
- Claim the website.
- Claim Instagram on Pinterest.
- Create or clean up 10 boards based on buyer search intent.

### Days 5-8: Content buildout

- Pick the 5 strongest projects.
- Create or update website pages for those projects.
- Create 5 Pins per project.
- Prepare 3 Instagram Reels and 2 carousels from the same project material.

### Days 9-11: Publish and connect

- Publish Pins with direct links to the relevant website pages.
- Publish Instagram Reels and Stories with clear calls to action.
- Share project albums and website links on Facebook.
- Pin the 3 most important Instagram posts to the profile.

### Days 12-14: Test ads

- Run a small Meta traffic campaign to the strongest landing page.
- Test 2 to 3 ad creatives.
- Watch cost per landing page view and actual inquiries, not just likes.
- Keep the best ad and pause weak ones.

## 7. Metrics To Watch

- Website sessions by source
- Landing page views from Meta ads
- Pinterest outbound clicks
- Instagram profile visits and link clicks
- Facebook link clicks
- Contact form submissions
- Quote requests
- Purchases or deposits
- Cost per inquiry

## 8. Practical Posting Rhythm

- Instagram: 3 Reels, 2 feed posts, and Stories when active work is happening.
- Facebook: 3 posts per week, including at least one project album or client story.
- Pinterest: 5 to 10 fresh Pins per week.
- Website: 1 new portfolio, product, or article page every 1 to 2 weeks.

## 9. Key Takeaway

OchoWorks Designs should not just post more. It should build a repeatable traffic loop:

1. Publish the best work on the website.
2. Turn each project into Instagram and Facebook proof.
3. Turn each project into multiple Pinterest search assets.
4. Track what sends real visitors and inquiries.
5. Put small ad dollars behind the best-performing content.

The strategy is simple: Instagram creates interest, Facebook builds trust, Pinterest compounds traffic, and the website converts that attention into business.
